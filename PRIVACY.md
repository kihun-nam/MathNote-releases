# MathNote — Privacy Policy

*Effective date: 7 May 2026*

## Overview

MathNote is a document editor that runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

## Data that stays on your device

- **Documents.** Your Markdown and LaTeX files are stored locally and, if you enable iCloud, synced via Apple's iCloud Drive. MathNote never reads or uploads your documents to any server it operates.
- **Photos.** Camera and Photo Library access is used solely to insert images into your documents. Photos are not transmitted anywhere.
- **Subscription status.** In-app purchase and subscription state is managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. MathNote does not process or store payment information.

## Ask AI

The Ask AI feature bundles your note into a PDF and presents the iOS share sheet. You choose which app receives the PDF — for example Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini. MathNote itself does not connect to any AI service and does not send your data to any third party. The privacy policy of the app you share to governs how that app handles the data.

## Analytics and tracking

MathNote does not include any analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

## Third-party services

MathNote does not integrate with any third-party services. The only network activity is iCloud document sync, handled by the operating system.

## Children's privacy

MathNote does not knowingly collect information from children. The app contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.
